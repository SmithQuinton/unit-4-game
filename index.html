Steven Universe

Crystal Collectors!

Help Steven and the Crystal Gems, collect the correct
amount of crystals.

You will be given a random number at the start of the
game.

By clicking on Garnet, Steven, Amethyst, or Pearl, you
will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the number
given. You lose the if your total score goes above the number given.

The value of each Crystal Gem is hidden from you until you click on it.

Win or Lose, the game will change the value of the total
you need to reach, as well as the value of each Crystal Gem.
